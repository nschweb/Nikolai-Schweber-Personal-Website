About

Nikolai Schweber was born in Los Angeles, CA in 2000. Growing up, he was fascinated by science, especially dinosaurs. He also liked Star Wars and Legos.

In high school, Nikolai played for the tennis team and was on leadership. He also volunteered at the Museum of Tolerance.

After high school, Nikolai went on to attend college at the Universtiy of California, Berkeley where he is a rising third-year student. In college, Nikolai is majoring in Political Science and Psychology. This summer, he is completing the summer minor in Journalism. He also volunteer coaches youth tennis and was recently hired as a video producer for the ASUC student government.
